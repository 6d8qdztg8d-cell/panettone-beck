Our Philosophy

Patient Fermentation.
Exceptional Flavor.

We believe that bread is a living thing. By using only three base ingredients—flour, water, and sea salt—we allow time to do the heavy lifting. Our 48-hour fermentation process breaks down gluten and unlocks the complex sugars in the grain, resulting in a loaf that is as nutritious as it is delicious.
